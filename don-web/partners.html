Why partner with Pact

Adopt an evidence standard without becoming the judge, escrow, or enforcement layer.

  • No liability absorption. You emit or verify evidence; you don't adjudicate disputes or hold funds.
  • Verifier-first. Ship proof that anyone can check offline — no dependency on your runtime or dashboard.
  • Institution-ready. Packs produce GC PDFs, insurer summaries, and audit-grade artifacts your customers already expect.
  • Clear responsibility. Default Blame Logic gives a deterministic answer (responsible actor or NO_FAULT) so disputes resolve from evidence, not debate.

What they get

How partnering works

Provide accountability without becoming the judge.

Neutral by design

Pact is not a marketplace, custodian, or arbiter. Partners can ship Pact evidence without owning disputes, outcomes, or enforcement.

Liability containment

When something goes wrong, Pact produces third-party-verifiable proof. Partners provide evidence — not explanations, opinions, or legal conclusions.

Verifier-only adoption path

Partners can adopt Pact incrementally. Start by emitting or accepting Auditor Packs; full SDK integration is optional and non-blocking.

Standards, not lock-in

Pact artifacts are portable, inspectable, and replayable. Partners are not locked into a SaaS platform, runtime, or proprietary review system.

Pact partners ship trust without absorbing risk.

Pact partners do not resolve disputes. They provide evidence strong enough that disputes resolve themselves.
