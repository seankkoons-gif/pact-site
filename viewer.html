Evidence Viewer

Local, offline, drag-drop Auditor Pack. Designed for counsel and underwriters. No server. No account. Drop a pack, see verdict, timeline, DBL panel, and export GC or Insurer PDFs.

DBL panel

Default Blame Logic result and responsible actor (if any).

Warnings

None, or list of non-fatal advisories.

Export GC PDF Export Insurer PDF
